Overview

Hexlume Games ("we", "us", or "our") built Sudoku 9 as a free-to-play mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

By downloading or using Sudoku 9 you agree to this policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the app.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Device & Technical Data: Device model, operating system version, app version, language, and general location (country/region). This data is collected automatically through our advertising and analytics partners.
  • Usage Data: In-app behavior such as sessions played, difficulty levels selected, and feature interactions — used solely to improve gameplay experience.
  • Advertising Identifiers: With your consent, Google AdMob may collect advertising identifiers (such as the Android Advertising ID) to serve personalized advertisements.
  • Game Progress (Local Only): Your puzzle progress, statistics, and settings are stored exclusively on your device using localStorage. We do not transmit this data to any server.

We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, precise GPS location, or any other personally identifiable information directly.

  • To display advertisements through Google AdMob (banner, interstitial, and rewarded video ads).
  • To monitor app stability and diagnose technical issues via crash reporting.
  • To analyze aggregate gameplay patterns and improve future game features.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

On first launch, you will be asked to provide or deny consent for personalized advertising. If you decline, non-personalized (contextual) ads will be shown instead. You can change your preference at any time in the app's Settings → Privacy screen.
